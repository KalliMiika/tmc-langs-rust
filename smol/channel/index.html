[−][src]Module smol::channel

An async multi-producer multi-consumer channel.

There are two kinds of channels:

  1. [Bounded][bounded()] channel with limited capacity.
  2. [Unbounded][unbounded()] channel with unlimited capacity.

A channel has the [Sender] and [Receiver] side. Both sides are cloneable and can be shared among multiple threads.

When all [Sender]s or all [Receiver]s are dropped, the channel becomes closed. When a channel is closed, no more messages can be sent, but remaining messages can still be received.

The channel can also be closed manually by calling [Sender::close()] or [Receiver::close()].

Examples

let (s, r) = async_channel::unbounded();

assert_eq!(s.send("Hello").await, Ok(()));
assert_eq!(r.recv().await, Ok("Hello"));
